Eco-Saver Bricks: Building a Sustainable Future
From Waste Materials

At Eco-Saver Bricks, we are committed to revolutionizing construction by utilizing waste plastic, old tires, and fabric tiles to create eco-friendly, durable bricks. Our mission is to reduce waste, lower carbon footprints, and provide a sustainable alternative to traditional building materials. Our bricks are not only environmentally friendly but also highly durable and cost-effective, making them an ideal choice for green construction projects.


Step by Step Process

1

Collection and Sorting

Plastic waste is collected and sorted to remove contaminants and non-recyclable materials.

2

Shredding

The sorted plastic is shredded into small pieces for uniformity in processing.

3

Mixing with Aggregates

Shredded plastic is mixed with aggregates like sand to enhance structural integrity.

4

Heating and Molding

The mixture is heated, melted, and poured into molds for shaping. Cooling solidifies the bricks.
